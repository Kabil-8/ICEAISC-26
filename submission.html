Instructions for authors and paper submission

Instructions to Authors

Important Notice

Original contributions of ongoing research work are expected from the authors. Papers will be reviewed by experts and selected based on novelty and technical content.

Paper Format Requirements

  • Papers must be in IEEE format
  • Maximum length: 6 pages (including references and appendices)
  • Submitted as soft-copy (docx format recommended)
  • Must include correspondence address, contact number and email address
  • All accepted papers must be presented orally by one of the authors

Submission Process

  1. Prepare your manuscript according to IEEE format (max 6 pages)
  2. Ensure all author details and contact information are included
  3. Send the complete paper as docx attachment to: iceaisc26@gmail.com
  4. Review process takes approximately 2-3 weeks
  5. Notification of acceptance will be sent by March 14, 2026
