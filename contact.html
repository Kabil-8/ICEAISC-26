Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where should I submit my paper?

Please submit your full paper in IEEE format to iceaisc26@gmail.com. Make sure to include all author details and contact information.

2. What is the registration fee and what does it include?

Registration fees range from Rs. 7,000 to Rs. 9,000 depending on category. The fee includes conference kit, certificate, access to all sessions and hospitality. Accommodation is arranged separately at nominal charges.

3. How will I know if my paper is accepted?

Acceptance notifications will be sent via email by March 14, 2026. Please ensure the email address provided during submission is correct and check your spam folder.

4. Can I submit multiple papers?

Yes, you can submit multiple papers. However, authors with multiple accepted papers are required to register separately for each paper to ensure inclusion in the conference proceedings and schedule.

5. How can I contact the organizing committee?

You can reach the organizing committee through the contact numbers provided above or by email at iceaisc26@gmail.com. The committee is available during business hours on weekdays.

For more questions, please refer to the submission guidelines or contact us directly using the information above.
