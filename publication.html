PUBLICATION OPPORTUNITIES

Disseminating research from ICEAISC'26

Publication Process

All manuscripts submitted to ICEAISC'26 will undergo a rigorous peer-review process, ensuring the quality and integrity of the research presented. The review process is double-blind, with at least two independent reviewers evaluating each submission.

Selection Criteria

Papers will be selected based on:

  • Originality and novelty of the research
  • Technical quality and correctness
  • Clarity of presentation
  • Relevance to conference themes
  • Significance of results

Publication Venues

Selected papers from ICEAISC'26 will be considered for publication in the regular or special issues of the following journals:

Journal of Engineering and Technology for Industrial Applications

An interdisciplinary journal focusing on engineering applications and technological innovations for industrial advancement.

ISSN: 2447-0228 Indexed Peer-Reviewed

Journal of Science and Technology Research

A multidisciplinary journal publishing original research in science and technology with broad applications.

ISSN: 2630-3916 Open Access Peer-Reviewed

Important Note

The papers will be processed as per the policies of the respective journals. Article Processing Charges (APC) may be applicable as per journal norms. Authors of selected papers will receive specific instructions regarding publication procedures after the conference.

Submit for Publication Consideration

Publication Ethics & Copyright

Author Responsibilities

  • Ensure originality of the submitted work
  • Properly cite all referenced works
  • Disclose any conflicts of interest
  • Participate in the peer review process
  • Correct errors when discovered

Copyright Policy

  • Authors retain copyright of their work
  • Grant publishing rights to journal
  • Allow sharing with proper attribution
  • Follow Creative Commons licenses where applicable

Plagiarism Policy: All submissions will be checked for plagiarism. Papers with significant overlap with previously published work will be rejected. The conference follows COPE (Committee on Publication Ethics) guidelines.
