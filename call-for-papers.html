CALL FOR PAPERS

Submit your original research to ICEAISC'26

Conference Scope

Authors are encouraged to submit original research articles related to the following thrust areas. The conference welcomes submissions on cutting-edge research in intelligent computing technologies, though submissions are not limited to the topics listed below.

ICEAISC'26 focuses on research that enables intelligence closer to data sources, enhances real-time decision-making, and supports scalable, efficient and secure smart systems. We invite papers that present novel algorithms, architectures, systems, and applications in the following domains:

Thrust Areas & Topics

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Artificial Intelligence Machine Learning Deep Learning Neural Networks Data Mining Quantum Computing

Computing & Networking

Edge and Fog Computing Cloud Computing Distributed Computing Ubiquitous Computing Internet of Things Wireless Sensor Networks

Security & Blockchain

Cyber Security Network Security Ethical Hacking Block Chain Technologies

Communication & Systems

Communication and Networking Smart Computing Systems Intelligent Analytics Real-time Systems

Target Audience

The conference welcomes participation from:

Faculty Members Research Scholars UG/PG Students Industry Delegates Industry Professionals
