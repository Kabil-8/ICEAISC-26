Dr. Mahalingam College of Engineering and Technology, Pollachi, Tamil Nadu

About the Venue

Dr. Mahalingam College of Engineering and Technology

MCET was established in 1998 by Dr. M. Manickam to commemorate the 75th birth year of his father Arutchelvar Dr. N. Mahalingam. The institution has grown to become a premier engineering college in the region with excellent infrastructure and academic facilities.

The conference will be held in the main auditorium and department seminar halls, equipped with state-of-the-art audio-visual facilities, high-speed internet, and comfortable seating for participants.
